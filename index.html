Visit Mehamn

Discover Mehamn's breathtaking Arctic charm nestled along Norway's northern coast.

Top three activities to do in Mehamn

Northern lights viewing

Mehamn's Arctic skies offer ideal conditions during winter nights to experience the unforgettable views of the northern lights.

Exploring Nature and Hiking

Mehamn is surrounded by breathtaking natural landscapes, including rugged cliffs, fjords, and scenic coastal trails.

Arctic Fishing Adventures

Partake in guided fishing excursions to experience the thrill of catching Arctic char, cod, halibut, and other local species.
